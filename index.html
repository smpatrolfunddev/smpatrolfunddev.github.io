Stratton Patrol Fund

Stratton Patrol Fund | Stratton, VT

The Patrol Fund is a nonprofit and all donations are tax deductible.

The fund supports the local Stratton community along with emergent needs of our patrol family.

Our mission is to provide monetary support to the Stratton Ski & Safety Patrols for the purpose of emergent needs of our members, acknowledge service accomplishments, recognize meritorious actions, support education & training, and to assist our local community.
